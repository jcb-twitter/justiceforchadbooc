Data Analysis

As we have our preprocessed data, we proceed to analyze the data and answer our hypothesis.

Data Modeling

To determine and analyze the reasons why Chad Booc is red-tagged by Twitter accounts, we clustered our 151 preprocessed tweets, using natural language prcocessing (NLP), into different topics using linear discriminant analysis (LDA) and t-Distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding (t-SNE).

To give a brief background,
LDA is a topic modeling technique used to extract topics from a given corpus
t-SNE is an unsupervised machine learning algorithm for visualization.

Figure 1: Topic clustering using LDA and t-SNE

These clusters describe the top reasons why Chad Booc is red-tagged on Twitter:

  • Topic 1 associates Chad Booc with being an NPA recruiter, who was killed in an encounter.
  • Topic 2 associates Chad Booc with being a communist and a rebel.
  • Topic 3 associates Chad Booc with multiple things: Hitler, Barabas, Satanas, the Akbayan Partylist, and being a public servant.
  • Topic 4 associates Chad Booc with being a terrorist.
  • Topic 5 associates Chad Booc with false beliefs and radical ideologies, and doubts on factual news articles about him.

Statistical Test

To show that there is a dominant topic in the Twitter space that red-tagged Chat Booc, the chi-square test was performed to check if there is a significant difference in the number of tweets among all topics. We have the following hypotheses:

H0 = The number of tweets per cluster does not have a significant difference with each other.

H1 = The number of tweets per cluster does have a significant difference with each other.

Since the chi-square value (19.56) is greater than the critical value (13.28) at a 99% confidence interval and the p-value (0.0006) is also much smaller than the confidence level, we reject the null hypothesis. Therefore, there is a significant difference in the number of tweets among all clusters.

It can then be inferred that Topic 1 stands out with a significantly higher number of tweets compared to the other topics.

Significantly more tweets that red-tag Chad Booc accuse him of being an NPA recruiter.

Additionally, from the figure above, the 2nd most common topic accuses Chad Booc of being a terrorist. And the top 3 topic associates Chad Booc with being a communist and a rebel.

Conclusion

Chad Booc was red-tagged by Twitter accounts. They were accusing him of being an NPA recruiter, a terrorist, a communist, a rebel. He was also accused of carrying false beliefs & radical ideologies. Tweets also spread doubts on factual news about him.

The results are heartbreaking for the team. Chad’s selfless heart to fight for the underserved should have never been met with such baseless accusations, or any form of injustice—let alone violence.
