Problem Formulation

To start off our project, we analyze the problems behind our chosen topic.

Research Question

 What are the multiple reasons Twitter accounts have to red-tag human rights activist Chad Booc and label him as a terrorist or communist?

Hypothesis

 Twitter accounts red-tag Chad Booc because Lumad teachers are accused of terrorist or communist activities.

Null Hypothesis

 There are no apparent reasons for Twitter accounts to red-tag a Lumad teacher.

Action Plan

 Determine and analyze the reasons why Chad Booc is red-tagged by Twitter accounts.
