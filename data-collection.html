Data Collection

To obtain our dataset, we mined Twitter for tweets containing specific keywords about Chad Booc.

Topic

We gathered tweets that specifically red-tag Chad Booc and label him as a terrorist or communist.

Tools

For the automated mining of tweets, we used snscraper, which is a scraper for social networking services such as Twitter.

Keywords

We mined tweets that can be found when the following keywords are searched:

  • chad booc npa
  • chad booc communist
  • chad booc terrorista
  • chad booc recruit

Only tweets posted from January 1, 2016 to December 31, 2022 were collected.

We obtained a total of 151 tweets for the dataset!
